Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

Introduction

At Fera Tools, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools.

Data Collection

Fera Tools operates with a strict privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data that you input into our tools. All processing occurs entirely within your browser on your local device. We do not require registration, account creation, or any form of personal identification to use our services.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies solely for essential functionality, such as storing your theme preference (dark/light mode) locally in your browser. These cookies do not contain any personal information and are used exclusively to enhance your user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain features like theme persistence.

Third-Party Services

Some tools may utilize third-party APIs or services for enhanced functionality (such as external APIs for certain calculations or conversions). When using these tools, your data may be processed by these third-party services according to their respective privacy policies. However, we strive to minimize third-party dependencies and prioritize client-side processing whenever possible.

Analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand website usage patterns and improve our services. This information is aggregated and anonymized and does not identify individual users.

Data Security

Since all processing happens client-side, your data never leaves your device unless explicitly required by a specific tool feature. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect our website infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
